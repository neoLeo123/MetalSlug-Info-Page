METAL SLUG

About Index Info Reviews

Metal Slug is a classic 2D side-scrolling run-and-gun arcade game developed by Nazca Corporation and later by SNK. It was first released in 1996. The game follows the adventures of a group of elite soldiers as they battle through various enemy-filled levels to thwart the plans of a sinister organization known as the Rebel Army.

Players take on the role of one of the protagonists, Marco or Tarma, and in later series the game added two additional playable characters Eri and Fio. Player must navigate through challenging terrain, defeat hordes of enemy soldiers, tanks, helicopters, and other deadly machines. Along the way, players can pick up power-ups, new weapons, and vehicles like tanks or armored vehicles to aid them in their mission.

Metal Slug is known for its fast-paced action, vibrant graphics, and humorous animations. It also features cooperative multiplayer, allowing two players to team up and face the enemies together, making it a popular choice in arcades and among retro gaming enthusiasts. The series has seen multiple sequels and spin-offs on various platforms, contributing to its enduring popularity in the gaming community.

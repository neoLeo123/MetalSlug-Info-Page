About Me

My name is Leo Ru, and I am an ambitious and determined student at Seneca College, where I am currently pursuing my passion for computer programming. While my journey in education began with a bachelor's degree in Kinesiology from York University, I soon realized that my heart lies in the realm of technology and programming. Driven by a desire for personal growth and a fulfilling career, I took a leap of faith and enrolled at Seneca to embark on a new path.
Choosing to create a website dedicated to Metal Slug holds a profound significance for me. This classic 2D side-scrolling run-and-gun arcade game not only brings back cherished memories from my childhood but also represents the spirit of camaraderie and joy I shared with my sibling while playing it together. By creating this website, I aim to showcase my technical prowess and creative skills while paying tribute to a game that has left an indelible mark on my life.
In this journey of self-discovery and professional growth, I am excited to merge my love for programming with my innate curiosity, always pushing the boundaries to create impactful and engaging digital experiences. With each line of code I write, I am reminded of the limitless possibilities that lie ahead, and I am committed to making a positive difference in the world through the power of technology.
